About Our Founder

In May 2022, Ayaan – a trained actor from Regent’s University London, UK, gave shape to the ambitions brimming in his mind and green-lighted Verssatile Solutions, a communications consultancy.

A brief history of his professional journey. Since 2015, Ayaan has sharpened his entrepreneurial skills at three diverse positions.

He functioned as the Co-Founder and Director of a company specialising in filmmaking and media studies curriculums and workshops teaching rural and urban children across India from 2015 to 2020. 

Co-founding his second start-up in 2020 : a Bollywood NFT marketplace, he functioned as the Head of Partnerships & Content until 2021, adding expertise by incorporating iconic moments from Bollywood cinema onto the Blockchain. 

Since 2021, Ayaan has been engaging actively with conscious fashion in his capacity as the acting brand custodian of an Indian MultiNational clothing brand, and parallelly overseeing the growth of his current startup, Verssatile Solutions.
